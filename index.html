Mariajosé Zapatier Q. —
I

Some brands speak loudly.
The ones I love
don't have to.

II

I come from Ecuador.

I grew up understanding that beauty doesn't announce itself. It just exists.

III

I built my career
in Buenos Aires —

Inside complex systems. Banking, retail, payment ecosystems. Learning how to protect an identity when a thousand people are trying to dilute it.

IV

That's where I found
my obsession.

The discipline of restraint. Knowing what to keep. Knowing what to remove.

V

There's a brand I've understood
since I was young.

Not because I studied it. Because I wore it. Because it taught me, before I had words for it, that less is the most powerful thing you can say.

VI

I moved to
the Netherlands.

To work closer to the brands that think the way I do.

VII

Mariajosé Zapatier Q.

I build visual systems that protect what's essential —
across markets, at scale,
without losing the silence
that makes a brand worth looking at.
